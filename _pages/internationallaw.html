Know Your Defensive and Offensive Documentation Rights by Country

Many countries have laws that extend protection to international travelers in cases of sexual misconduct and have a reporting process you can take advantage of. It is through these processes that we can affect change and elevate people to positions of power who support our right to safety and respect to replace those who dont.

This resource provides information about laws related to recording, consent for photographs/videos/audio, legal documentation rights, and other relevant protections during fieldwork by country.

Disclaimer: This is not an exhaustive or authoritative legal resource. Laws are subject to change. While this website is designed to update automatically as the original source websites update, you should still contact university legal services or a qualified legal advisor to confirm the most current regulations before conducting fieldwork.
